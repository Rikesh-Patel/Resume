Mission Statement

Francis Bacon once said, "Knowledge is power." Now, centuries later, I say data is power. From my first moments with a computer in elementary school, I've had a fondness for number games. Games with rows upon rows of digits itched the quantitative side of my brain, and to this day, I can still enumerate my favorites. Essentially to win the game, I needed to make the numbers larger, so I did everything from Google sheets to Excel, from putting a weight on my mouse to Python-scripting and macros; if it made me more money, saved time, or got an achievement in the game, I would do it. The peak of this fascination was clicking ‘Start’ and then lying back in my chair as some controlled authority took ahold of my screen.

Without knowing it, this past time would lead me up the path of data modeling. This is all part of my grand scheme—to mark these efficiencies, lionize strengths, snip weaknesses for the most elaborate game of them all. Life. To me, data unveils a new dimension with limitless potential. Imagine the possibilities. I love creating, editing videos, writing story lines, and designing graphics, so with my foundation in Econometrics and working experience with data visualization, I yearn to make great strides.

I live for it.
